Guacamole

Description

Guacamole!

Directions

  1. Take out the pulp of the avocados
  2. Cut the onions, tomatoes, and coriander in very small pieces
  3. Put everything in a bowl.
  4. Add salt, lime and olive oil
  5. Mix and serve. Maybe chill before eating.
